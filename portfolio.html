About

I'm Surya Prakash Sah, a computer engineer who likes building things twice — once to make them work, and once to make them fast and correct. My path runs from a small school in Sarlahi to college in Kathmandu, to a B.Tech in Bhubaneswar, and it shaped how I work: comfortable moving between contexts, and used to figuring things out with whatever's on hand.

Day to day, I build for the web — interfaces, APIs, the full stack in between — and I spend the rest of my time on AI/ML, training and shipping models rather than just reading about them. I like projects where both sides meet: intelligent systems wrapped in interfaces people can actually use.
